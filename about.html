About me

As a kind, energetic and unconditionally positive person, I focus on strengths and solutions to help individuals, teams and organizations discover, design and deliver their dreams.

As an Appreciative Interventionist I work with individuals (coaching) and groups (training). I help them to intervene and create their dreams. I focus on strengths, solutions and appreciate what is already there. The methods I use are consistent with my outlook on life; methods such as appreciative inquiry, solution focussed brief therapy, positive psychology and a lot of creativity. I’m sure you will love it just as much as I do!

I studied at the University of Amsterdam and hold a master's degree in social psychology as well as a master's degree in the science of communication. I’m a certified Interventionist from the Institute of Interventionism in the Netherlands. My studies provided a theoretical foundation to my belief in the power of teams. The magic of individuals working together, ‘in flow’, co-creating and reaching the most amazing goals is what ignites my passion for my work!

I grew up in the Netherlands, spend some time in France and now enjoy living in San Francisco. Just as different countries have different cultures, so do organizations. I see it as an adventure to help organizations and the people who form them, by creating a culture that helps everybody to thrive.

It’s my dream to live a happy, loving and fulfilling life, to make a blissful contribution to the world as a trainer/coach and to continue to reach my full potential in doing so.

I love to find out what makes you blossom and grow, and to find out where there is room for you in order to grow even more into your best self. Set up an 1 hour free introduction to see how my services can help you realize your dream.
